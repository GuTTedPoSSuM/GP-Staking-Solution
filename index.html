Earn While Offline

GPSS is a secure and reliable method for staking Ethereum. Earn a competitive 3.1% APY on your staked Ethereum, paid in Ethereum at the end of the staking term as specified in the agreement. Even if the GPSS goes offline for network maintenance or system upgrades, your staking will be minimally affected. In such cases, rewards will be based on the duration of the network failure and will be paid in $RBR coin (Rubioberry).
